Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What services do you offer?

A: We offer vehicle sales, rentals, and assistance with selling cars.

Q: How do I book a car rental?

A: You can book through our website by visiting the Hire a Car section.

Q: What are your office hours?

A: Our office hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Q: Do you require a deposit for rentals?

A: Yes, a security deposit is required at the time of rental, which will be refunded upon return of the vehicle in good condition.

Q: Can I extend my rental period?

A: Yes, you can extend your rental period. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to arrange this.

Q: What happens if I return the car late?

A: Late returns may incur additional charges. Please inform us if you expect to return the vehicle later than agreed.

Q: Do you offer roadside assistance?

A: Yes, we provide 24/7 roadside assistance for all our rental vehicles.

Q: What is the minimum age requirement to rent a vehicle?

A: The minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21 years. Drivers under 25 may incur a young driver surcharge.

Q: Are there mileage limits on rentals?

A: We offer unlimited mileage on most rentals, but please check the specific terms for your chosen vehicle.

Q: Can I drive the rental car outside the country?

A: Driving outside the country is allowed with prior approval. Additional insurance may be required.

Q: What should I do if the vehicle breaks down?

A: Contact our roadside assistance immediately for help. Do not attempt to repair the vehicle yourself.

Q: Can I change my reservation after booking?

A: Yes, you can modify your reservation up to 24 hours before your rental period starts.

Q: Do you have child seats available for rent?

A: Yes, child seats are available for an additional fee. Please request them at the time of booking.

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

A: We accept major credit cards and debit cards. Cash payments are not accepted for rentals.

Q: Is insurance included in the rental price?

A: Basic insurance is included, but we recommend purchasing additional coverage for better protection.

Q: How can I sell my car to Melvin's Wheels and Deals?

A: You can sell your car by visiting our dealership and providing details about your vehicle. We'll assess its value and make you an offer.

Q: What documents do I need to sell my car?

A: You will need the vehicle's title, registration, and your identification. Any maintenance records are also helpful.

Q: Do you offer trade-in options?

A: Yes, we offer trade-in options when you purchase a vehicle from us. We will assess your current vehicle's value.

Q: What types of vehicles are available for sale?

A: We have a diverse inventory, including sedans, SUVs, trucks, and luxury cars. Visit our sales section for current listings.

Q: Can I get financing for my vehicle purchase?

A: Yes, we offer financing options through our partners. Speak to our sales team for more details.

Q: How do I know if a used car is in good condition?

A: All our used cars undergo a thorough inspection. You can also request a vehicle history report for transparency.

Q: What happens if I have issues with my purchased vehicle?

A: We stand behind our vehicles. If you encounter any issues, contact us, and we will assist you with repairs or concerns.
