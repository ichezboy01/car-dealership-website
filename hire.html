Requirements for Hiring a Vehicle

To ensure a smooth hiring process, please bring the following documents when you visit us:

  • A valid driver's license (must be held for at least 2 years).
  • A national ID or passport for identification.
  • Proof of residence (such as a utility bill).
  • A valid credit card for payment and security deposit.
  • Minimum age requirement: 21 years.
